Mood Check Privacy Policy

Mood Check is designed to help users track mood, PMS symptoms, and PMDD-related patterns over time.

Information We Collect

Mood Check stores the information you enter in the app, such as daily symptom ratings, notes, cycle day, period tracking data, and optional Apple sign-in session information.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s tracking, history, trends, and export features.

Data Storage

Your entries are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export them. Exported CSV files are created only when you request an export and are then handled according to where you save or share them.

Sign In with Apple

Mood Check offers optional Sign in with Apple. If you choose to sign in, the app may store basic account information needed to recognize your session.

Data Sharing

Mood Check does not sell your personal data. Mood Check does not share your symptom data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Your Choices

You can use the app as a guest, export your data as a CSV file, or delete your account and local data at any time from inside the app.

Children’s Privacy

Mood Check is not directed to children under 13.
